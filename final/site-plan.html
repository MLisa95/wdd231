Qonce Chamber of Commerce

Purpose of the Site

The purpose of this site is to encourage informal and small businesses to nurture and grow their businesses. It is also to provide insightful information about the businesses such as contact information and about the city itself.

Target Marget

Business owners, potentisl business owners, citizens of Qonce, and visitors of the Qonce city.

Goals

  • Encourage more people to start and grow their own businesses, even if they are informal.
  • Strengthen small growing businesses.
  • Provide marketing information on businesses.

Users of the Site

Informal Business: A 38 year-old woman who lives in a house of 6, where she is the only one providing for her family.

Small Business: A 23 year-old male who was selling muffins at school and recently opened up a small bakery from home.

Scenarios

  • A local business owner is looking for resources that are available to help make her business formal so she can qualify for business funding.
  • A calendar is available to business owners of current and upcoming business events such as mmarketplaces, expos, grand openings and trainings.
