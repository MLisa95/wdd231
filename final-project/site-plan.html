MY SITE PLAN

Site Name: Mr. E. Tutoring Services

I chose to keep the original name of my previous workplace because the owner of the school is the actual brand. He worked tirelessly to build his own school and was loved by his students so much they started calling him "Mr E".

Site Purpose:

The site provides tutoring services to scholars in Grades 4 to 12. Tutoring services can also be in the form of one-on-one to cater to scholars who need more intimate or special teaching.

Scenarios:

  • Which subjects do you offer tutoring in for Grade 12?
  • How well do students perform after attending the tutoring school?
  • Can I apply online without visiting the school?
  • Do you offer any online tutoring services?
  • Do you provide progress reports?
