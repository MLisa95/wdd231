Our Mission

At Mr. E Extra Tuition, we seek to inspire and instil confidence in every student. Learning is intentional, and our results reflect that commitment. We carefully select passionate tutors who are experts in their subjects and dedicated to nurturing each learner's growth. Mr. E Extra Tuition boasts a proud history of excellence and lasting relationships with our clients — because we understand that students are not just statistics, but individuals with unique potential, dreams, and stories waiting to be written.

Our Story

Before founding Mr. E Extra Tuition, Mr. E gained experience working at several tutoring centers, juggling multiple roles while refining his teaching approach. It was during this time that students affectionately began calling him "Mr. E," a nickname that quickly stuck. In 2019, he had his breakthrough, attracting a massive following of students who appreciated his teaching style. Inspired by this support, he established his own school under the same name, bringing his vision of high-quality, personalized tutoring to life. The school quickly became a sensation, earning a reputation for excellence and helping countless students achieve their academic goals. Learn more...
