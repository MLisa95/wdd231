About Me

My name is Lisakhanya Masiza (maiden surname Gasa), and I'm from South Africa. I live in the 2nd biggest township, Mdantsane, which is beautifully built on hills and streams. I love art in almost any form it comes in, be it painting, music or sandart. I went into programming because I wish to program children's apps to help them learn while they play.
